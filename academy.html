The 6-week program

30 hours · hybrid (live sessions + self-paced) · batches of 15–25 · corporate and individual enrolment

WEEK 1

AI Fundamentals & Mindset Shift

Demystify AI, kill the fear. Hands-on with ChatGPT, Claude and Gemini for real work tasks. Prompt engineering. Leave with a personal AI action plan — 3 tasks AI helps you with from day one.

WEEK 2

AI for Your Domain

Track-based training for your actual job — Finance, HR, Sales & Marketing, or Operations. You leave knowing the 5 AI tools most relevant to your role.

WEEK 3

Workflow Automation (No-Code)

Google Apps Script, Zapier/Make.com, and APIs at a conceptual level. You build 2 working automations you can use at work immediately.

WEEK 4

Data Literacy & Decision-Making

Pivot tables, dashboards, KPIs, AI-assisted analysis — and how to present data to management with confidence.

WEEK 5

Career Repositioning

Map your skills to AI-augmented roles. LinkedIn and resume rebuilt for the AI era, mock interviews, personal branding as an AI-ready professional.

WEEK 6

Capstone + Certification

Build a real automation or AI solution, present it to a panel, and graduate as an OTMOS Certified AI Professional.
