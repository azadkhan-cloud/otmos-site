What automates — and what stays human

Automation done right is not about replacing people. It's about removing the work nobody should be doing by hand.

Fully Automated

Documentation, data entry, tracking, reporting, reminders, reconciliation, invoice and letter generation — the repetitive 30–40% of everyone's week.

Partially Automated

Exception handling, escalations, review queues — the system does the heavy lifting, a human signs off.

Stays Human

Negotiation, judgment calls, relationships, sensitive conversations. By design, forever.
